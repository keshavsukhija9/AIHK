Analysis Methodology

Test Data Validation: All performance metrics calculated exclusively on unseen test data to ensure realistic evaluation and prevent overfitting bias.
Cross-Validation: 5-fold cross-validation used to assess model stability and generalization capability across different data splits.
Overfitting Detection: Models with CV-Test gap > 0.05 are flagged for potential overfitting issues that may affect real-world performance.
Industry Benchmarks: R² scores of 65-85% are considered excellent for real estate price prediction given market volatility and external factors.
